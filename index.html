About Rohan Interior

Merging Engineering Precision with Artistic Vision

Based in the heart of Thane at Ghodbunder Road, Rohan Interior and Engineers is more than just a decor firm. We are a team of qualified engineers and creative designers dedicated to transforming empty shells into vibrant homes, offices, and commercial labs.

Whether you own a 2BHK in Kasarvadavali, a Villa in Hiranandani Estate, or a Research Lab in Wagle Estate, our turnkey solutions ensure you don't have to deal with multiple vendors. We handle everything from civil changes to the final installation.

  • No Hidden Costs - Transparent BOQ
  • In-house Manufacturing & Quality Check
  • Dedicated Project Manager for Every Site
